Frequently Asked Questions

Have a different question about how we work or the pricing plans available? Get in touch with us.

Do you charge by the hour?

We avoid billing by the hour whenever it’s possible. It creates ambiguity and makes you worry about your upcoming CPA bill. The only time we are billing by the hour is when we cannot reasonably estimate our time spend on a specific non-recurring project.

Can I meet you in person? Afterall, you have my personal information!

We will be more than happy to meet you in person if you are in San Diego area. If not, we try to set up Zoom meetings for all of our new customers. Also, California CPAs can be verified on the CBA website. You are welcome to look us up! We are in compliance.

How do you deliver sensitive information to clients?

We are paperless and virtual. We utilize a secure client portal to exchange and deliver private information. Client documents are encrypted and stored on a secure server with two-factor authentication.

What else is included?

We believe in the “all included” approach. You are welcome to call us out of the blue, and we won’t charge for the conversation.

Can you give me a discount? I am broke.

Our accounting fees are already pretty low compared to other cannabis CPA firms. However, we do give some discounts to microbusinesses due to the large amount of accounting work required.

Can you prepare my 1040?

Yes we can! However, please keep in mind that individual tax preparation is not included in our business plans offered above.

Do you require a retainer?

We don’t ask for a retainer as long as you came to us from a different cannabis CPA firm. We trust our colleagues and believe in their accounting expertise. However, if prior to us you were doing your own accounting, or you are just starting out, we will ask for the retainer because of the amount of work that will be required to set up or clean up your accounting.
